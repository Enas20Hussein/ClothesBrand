How It Started

Our Dream is Global Learning Transformation

KawiX was founded by Robert Anderson, a passionate lifelong learner, and Maria Sanchez, a visionary educator. Their shared dream was to create a digital haven of knowledge accessible to all. Untouched by the belief in the transformational power of education, they embarked on a journey to build KawiX. With relentless dedication, they gathered a team of experts and launched this innovative platform, creating a global community of eager learners, all connected by the desire to grow and learn.
